The Orchard V1.0

At the top of the device you'll find a projector based device, this is to project a 3 Dimensional 'Environment' or visual immitation to appeal to the visual senses Close The device is also equipped with a speaker for ambient sounds played based on the environment chosen either by a preset chosen or by uploading your own memory. From here our AI finds related music and plays it at a comfortable volume Close Lastly it also functions as a lighting device when not in use or can be used to create ambient light not shown in the photo or video, this helps to add an extra layer to the memory experience. Our Mission

Smell is something often disregarded, people dont tend to consider it as a factor as it is expected that it is just, there.
However, we do not realise what life is like without smell until we have lost it,
so to help with that, we want to create a device to help remind people of the smells from different experiences that they have captured and kept close to their hearts.

What its like with no smell

You cannot experience the smell of a loved one, perfume, candles, of different environments or even yourself

You are unable to detect danger via smell, off-food or toxic products
making you more vulnerable to physical dangers

The neurological switch in your brain doesn't trigger between places as there is no smell trigger making it hard to relax,
